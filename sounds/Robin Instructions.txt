Perform the Syncing Gesture

Thoron - Double tap your middle finger with your thumb until
you feel a vibration. Hold your hand at your hip in a fist to 
charge, then push foreward with your fingers spread apart.

Arcfire - Double tap your middle finger with your thumb until
you feel a vibration. Hold your hand up in a fist. Flex your 
muscles for best results.

Nosferatu - Double tap your middle finger with your thumb until
you feel a vibration. Reach your hand out and wave inward as if 
trying to grab someone.

Switch Character - Double tap your middle finger with your thumb until
you feel a vibration. Wave your hand outward twice.
